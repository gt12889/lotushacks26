## Codebase Patterns
- SSE events in search.py use `yield f"data: {json.dumps(...)}\n\n"` format
- TinyFish SSE events have types: STARTED, STREAMING_URL, PROGRESS, HEARTBEAT, COMPLETE
- `search_single_pharmacy_safe` wraps `search_single_pharmacy` with timeout and retry logic
- `PharmacySearchResult.model_dump()` is used for serializing pharmacy results to SSE
- Frontend DashboardHome.tsx parses SSE events by checking `event.type` first, then falls back to `event.source_id` for pharmacy results
- `streaming_url_callback` pattern: pass a sync callback to async search functions for early event emission via asyncio.Queue
